# Content Lenses

Use these lenses when turning SocialSeal evidence into content direction.

## Aspirational

Creates emotion or mood. Good for destination desire, lifestyle fit, identity, atmosphere, or category excitement.

## Utility / practical

Teaches useful information or gives the viewer an unlock, relief, accomplishment, or confidence. Good for walkthroughs, comparisons, planning help, maps, steps, first-timer guidance, costs, timing, what to pack, what to expect, or how to choose.

For social search, utility content usually performs because it answers hesitation with practical detail.
